About

In short, I am a student seeking internship and co-op opportunities for the next four to twelve months.

Currently I am working for the Goverment of Canada as an Interaction Designer. What I like about my job is that I can work on user interfaces for real web applications that will be used by Canadians. I was also able to design an internal application out of user requirements by myself. That and I get to learn technology that is new to me, and work as part of a team on large applications that have been around for a while.

Most of the time I am a student at Carleton University, where I study Computer Science with a specialization in Mobile Computing. I'm interested in Human Computer Interaction, because I find the way people interact with computers to be unpredictable and exciting. I want to make intuitive user interfaces that are easy to use. I also have a minor in Greek and Roman Studies. Computer Science and Classics don't seem to go together (they didn't have computers in antiquity, did they?) but I found myself taking many of my electives in courses like Classical Mythology.

In my spare time, I find myself reading books out in the sunshine. I enjoy craft beer, though I can't explain in succinct words why I like certain beers and not others. Sometimes I pretend to be an artist and splash acrylic paint around on a canvas, but usually it just ends up being a mess. Other times I pretend to be a musician, and I practice playing the flute fairly regularly so it's not as much of a stretch. I am also an accomplished daydreamer.

 Lindsay Bangs 2015
